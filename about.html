Rooted in Psychology. Built for Change.

Ground Up Coaching is dedicated to transforming motivation into measurable, sustainable action using evidence-based behavioral science principles.

Our Coaching Philosophy

We move beyond simple goal-setting to address the underlying psychological and environmental factors that drive behavior. Our practice is guided by principles of Motivational Interviewing (MI), Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), and behavioral economics, ensuring strategies are practical and effective.

The Five Core Theoretical Foundations

Pillar 1: Intrinsic Motivation (MI)

Focuses on identifying and resolving ambivalence to change by eliciting the client's own reasons for moving forward.

Pillar 2: Values Clarification (ACT)

Ensuring goals are aligned with deep personal or organizational values to enhance commitment and resilience.
